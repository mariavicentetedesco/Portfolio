About Me

Hi! I'm Maria. I'm originally from Argentina, but I've been living in the UK for the last 8 years. At the age of 36 I decided I needed a new challenge, so here I am, learning to program and taking my chance at a career change!

I have always enjoyed studying new languages, so I see learning to code just like learning any other languages. I am bilingual in Spanish and English, and I have basic level of French and Portuguese. I've had many different jobs in various industries throughout my life, from tutoring to working in the public sector, hospitality and even finance. I've done it all, and I'm excited to try something new!

In my free time I enjoy travelling, reading and trying new cuisines from around the world.
